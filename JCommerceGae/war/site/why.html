Why gcShop?

Why gcShop?

  1. I am shop-owner in eBay.
  2. I have my own domain and rented server. I use other shopping-cart software.
  3. I used to be user of ECSHOP.
  4. I used to be ECSHOP's developer or customizing developer.
  5. I am a developer and I love Google technologies.
  6. My company or organization is interested to construct application with Google cloud computing platform.

Why gcShop?

I am shop-owner in eBay.

gcShop provide you customizable solution. You could implement your own functionalities and GUI style, which is impossible on eBay or other C2C/B2B web sites. There are already many shop owners used stand-alone shopping website. Most popular softwares in China are ShopEx and ECShop, while in US and Europe OSCommerce is popular.

I have my own domain and rented server. I use other shopping-cart software.

Did you experience network and server down-time? Did you ever be bothered with virus and hacker? Did you ever seen lost-of-users due to the down grade of user experience, which is caused by the server's performance bottleneck when number of users becomes large?

If you did, you could consider to try gcShop, gcShop is established on Google's powerful server technologies and services, which will save you from trouble to many extend. At the same time, thanks for Google's free policy and our free software, you needn't pay for it!

I used to be user of ECSHOP.

You could consider to try gcShop. In addition to enjoy the advantage of Google cloud computing platform, you might discover that gcShop had migrated or implemented many functionalities and operation styles from ECSHOP. It will be easy to start with, we even support the existing ECSHOP templates!

I used to be ECSHOP's developer or customizing developer.

ECSHOP has been acquired and started to charge. If you still like ECSHOP, welcome to participant in our gcShop. Your experience would give us great help.

I am a developer and I love Google technologies.

In gcShop, you would discover our effort on exploring Google technologies, e.g. Google App Engine, Google Web Toolkit, etc. Welcome to join us, or share with us your comments!

My company or organization is interested to construct application with Google cloud computing platform.

You could find that we had gathered a lot experience by practicing on Google App Engine. You could contact us to discuss further cooperation.
