General

What is gc-Shop?

gc-Shop is short name for "Google Cloud Shop". It is a shopping-cart or e-commerce software developed on Google's Cloud Computing platform: Google App Engine.

Why do we need another e-commerce application?

gc-Shop is different. Leveraging technologies and services of Google Cloud Computing, gc-Shop could be shop owner's better choice.

Besides the benefit of (see Google App Engine), gc-Shop is based on Google so it will be easier for us to integrate it with more Google services in the future, e.g. Google Check out, Google Talk, Google Product Search, Google AdSense, Google AdWords etc. We have more potential to become better.

read more about why gc-Shop?

How to start using gc-Shop?

Just a few simple steps:

  • Apply a free Google App Engine account from here, and create a new applicationID. You will own a free domain, like MyXXXShop.appspot.com.
  • Get a copy of gc-Shop, and run our install script to deploy it to your Google App Engine account.
  • Access your gc-Shop at MyXXXShop.appspot.com and start creating your goods items and other configuration.
  • Now you could enjoy as a shop Owner!

    How could potential clients find my online-shop?

    Need to understand that even though gc-Shop will try to be more search-engine friendly, you still have to spend effort to make their online-shops known.

    For those gc-Shop users who already own a successful online-shop, what you need to do is just link or redirect the flow from the old online-shop.

    For those new starter, gc-Shop will add features to integrate with Google Product Search, which will be helpful for clients to find you shop from Google Search Engine.

    With the power of Google search engine. We have reason to expect Google to provide better search support to Google App Engine in the future.

    Why did gc-Shop choose Google as the Cloud Computing platform among many other choices such as Amazon?

    Google App Engine is more friendly to starters as either role of application developer and application user.

  • Google provides a flexible billing policy, suite for all size of business.
  • Application on Google App Engine is easier to develop, deploy and manage.
  • Google provides a suite of tools/services which has the potential to be integrated to provide better user experience.
